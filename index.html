By folling these recommendations, you can signicantly reduce the risk of security breach on your website:
- strong secure password - should be at least 10 characters long. complex. use a compination of letters, numbers, cases and symbols to form. an unpredictable string of characters that does not resemble words or names.
- third-party solutions- install security plugins that can detec and block malicious traffic.
- keep your software up to date, this includes your CMS, plugins, themes, and any other softwae your site uses.
- Enable HTTPS: Use HTTPS to encrypt data transmission between your website information is not intercepted by attackers.
- Regulary test your website security.
Its importante to note that web security is an ongoing process that requires constant attention and adaptation to stay ahead of evolving threats.
